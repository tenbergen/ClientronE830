SpinRite II v2.0
Released in 1991 by Gibson Research
For DOS

Spinrite, by Gibson Research, is a tool that can diagnose, repair, 
and rejuvenate the low-level formatting and optimize the interleave 
of MFM and RLL (ST412/506 interface) hard disk drives. Its pattern 
testing ability is also useful for verifying the operation of SCSI 
and IDE hard drives.
